Social Outreach Platform

V-DAC is the social outreach platform of the IISE Berhampur Student community. Although it has been present for a long time, it has been rather inactive. Given the location of our institute we could be contributing so much more socially to the people around us. We are in a place of privilege and it's necessary to make sure that you give back to the society. The council will be organising various outreach activities this semester which will be largely student run.

Our Mission

Educational Outreach

Providing educational support and resources to underprivileged communities around our institute, bridging the knowledge gap through student volunteers.

Environmental Initiatives

Organizing environmental awareness programs and sustainability projects to create a greener future for our community.

Health & Wellness

Conducting health camps, awareness drives, and wellness programs to improve the quality of life in local communities.

Skill Development

Offering skill development workshops and training programs to empower individuals with practical knowledge and capabilities.

Social Awareness

Raising awareness about important social issues and mobilizing the student community for positive social change.

Community Partnerships

Building strong partnerships with local organizations, NGOs, and community leaders to amplify our impact.
